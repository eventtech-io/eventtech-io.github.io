Hedera

Licence-free wireless audio + data link designed to connect moving sound systems. Hedera delivers uncompressed stereo PCM audio with ultra-low latency, engineered specifically for hostile RF environments and moving floats.

What it does

  • Uncompressed stereo PCM audio @ 48 kHz
  • 3–5 ms AD/DA latency target
  • Licence-free RF operation
  • Designed for vibration, obstruction, and urban RF noise

Why it exists

Hedera was developed in direct response to real-world failures of traditional RF links during large-scale carnival deployments. The system prioritises predictable behaviour, engineer visibility, and graceful handling of packet loss.
