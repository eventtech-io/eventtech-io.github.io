Bespoke installations & services

Custom networked audio, control, and monitoring systems for venues that need modern infrastructure to be reliable, observable, and understandable on show day.

What we do

We design and deploy bespoke systems that combine professional audio, IP networking, and show control into a single, coherent backbone — tailored to your room, your shows, and your crew.

  • Custom AoIP and networked audio infrastructure for theatres, live music venues, studios, houses of worship, and broadcast spaces.
  • IP-based control layers (OSC and other open standards) that bridge audio, lighting, video, and automation hardware into one control surface.
  • Embedded monitoring and utility nodes for signal paths, system health, and at-a-glance status when the house is full and doors are open.
  • Clean documentation, drawings, and presets so day-to-day operation feels simple, even when the underlying system is not.

How we work

Installations are collaborative and engineer-led. We start from your existing inventory, workflows, and pain points, then design systems that add clarity rather than more boxes in the rack.

  • Discovery and audit of your current audio, network, and control infrastructure — what works, what breaks, and what nobody wants to touch.
  • Concept and detailed design, including network topology, audio routing, control architecture, and resilience strategies.
  • Staged installation and commissioning, with testing in real-world show conditions rather than only lab scenarios.
  • Training and handover for technical staff and visiting engineers, backed by clear, venue-specific documentation.

When this is useful

A bespoke installation is most valuable when reliability and repeatability matter more than one-off spectacle.

  • You are planning a new build or refurbishment and want a modern, IP-centric backbone from day one.
  • Your current system has grown organically and now feels fragile, opaque, or hard to support.
  • You need to integrate mixed-vendor equipment into a single, predictable workflow for in-house and visiting crews.
  • You want a long-term technical partner to evolve the system as your programme and technology change.
