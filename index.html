Research-led tools for the next generation of events.

Event Tech Research is a London-based R&D and education studio focused on modern event audio, networking, and control. We prototype practical tools for technicians and production teams, then share the knowledge back through documentation, training, and open-architecture design.

What we do

We explore how networked audio, OSC, and open protocols can simplify real-world workflows for engineers in live events, theatre, and broadcast.

  • Event-focused R&D and prototyping
  • Audio networking & control systems
  • Practical documentation and education

How we work

Our approach is grounded in field experience and transparent engineering.

  • Engineer-first design philosophy
  • Open standards over black boxes
  • Tested in real production environments
